About

Who am I

I am a graduate of Cardiff Metropolitan University with a 2:1 BSc (Hons) in Software Engineering. During my studies, I developed a strong interest in designing and building software that is clean, scalable and maintainable, with a focus on solving real-world problems through thoughtful engineering.

My strongest experience lies in Java, Spring Boot and SQL, where I enjoy designing software that's thoughtfully engineered, from backend architecture through to the user experience. I also enjoy creating intuitive user interfaces with HTML, CSS and JavaScript, ensuring the experience matches the quality and thought put into the underlying software.

I'm someone who enjoys understanding the 'why' behind the technology, not just the implementation. I explore how different technologies work together, finding thoughtful solutions to real-world problems. That mindset drives me to look beyond simply making software work and instead focus on building solutions that are well designed, maintainable and fit for purpose.
